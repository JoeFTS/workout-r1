HOW TO USE
THE DAILY WORKOUT

One circuit a day: push-ups, plank, squats, burpees — 3 to 5 rounds. Your rep targets come from your baseline test (about half your max per round).

BASELINE TEST

One max-effort set of each exercise. Dial in your number with the wheel, tap CONFIRM. Re-run it any time from the menu.

DURING A WORKOUT

Do the reps shown, then tap DONE. Did more or fewer? Dial the real number first — progress is based on what you actually did.

Plank: tap START HOLD and hold until the finish beeps. Tap STOP EARLY if you break — it logs the seconds you held.

Between rounds a 90-second rest counts down. Tap to skip it.

Side button steps back one exercise if you mis-tapped.

GETTING STRONGER

Finish 3+ workouts in a week and targets rise about 5%. Every third raise adds a round (max 5). Two empty weeks steps you back one — easing back in, never punishing.

RUN + SAUNA

LOG RUN: dial minutes; tap the MILES box to add distance.

SAUNA: START/STOP runs a live timer, or use MANUAL ENTRY to dial minutes after the fact.

STREAK

Any logged activity — workout, run, or sauna — keeps your daily streak alive.

CONTROLS

wheel = adjust / scroll
tap = confirm
side click = back or undo

SYNC

Green dot: history synced to your Google Sheet. Orange: entries queued (offline) — they send on their own, or tap RESYNC in the menu.
